Jo-Ann Arosemena Goldin

B. 1992, PANAMÁ CITY, PANAMÁ
LIVES AND WORKS IN BROOKLYN, NY

"I embrace liminality and tropicality. My work is characterized by organic abstraction, dreamscapes and poetry. I work predominantly by hand building ceramic clay. I have been exploring the realms of silversmithing and mineralogy to complement my ceramic work. My wish is to invite viewers to venture where the seen intertwines with the mysterious.  "
