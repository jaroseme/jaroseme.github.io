Jo-Ann Arosemena Goldin

B. 1992, PANAMÁ CITY, PANAMÁ
LIVES AND WORKS IN BROOKLYN, NY

"Immersed in the allure of deserts, oceans, and jungles, I am captivated by their unique and powerful landscapes.
My art explores the balance between fragility and strength, growth and restraint, often centered on the themes of folding and holding.
Embracing liminality, my work captures moments on the brink of reality and dreams, creating surreal dreamscapes that blur the boundaries of perception.
Symbolizing transitions and crossings between worlds, my art invites viewers to venture beyond the familiar into the unknown, where imagination and reality coexist, and the seen intertwines with the mysterious."
